Answers

Scrap calculator questions.

Are the scrap rates guaranteed?

No. The calculator provides indicative UAE guide values. Final quotes depend on inspection, sorting, purity, weight, transport, and buyer demand.

Which metals are included?

Copper, aluminium, brass, stainless steel, iron and steel, lead, zinc, batteries, electric motors, and insulated copper wire are included in the first version.

How are currency conversions updated?

The site uses a free no-key currency endpoint when available and falls back to the most recent stored exchange values if the endpoint is unavailable.

Can this become fully live for UAE scrap prices?

Yes. The site reads scrap rates from a JSON feed. Updating that feed daily, manually or through a later backend task, updates the website without redesigning the page.

Why can a buyer quote differ from the calculator?

The calculator does not inspect material. Buyers may adjust prices for dirt, plastic, insulation, moisture, mixed metals, transport cost, minimum quantity, and the exact grade received.

Should I send photos before pickup?

Yes. Clear photos of the material, quantity, and storage location help a buyer estimate grade, loading access, and pickup requirements before visiting.
