FAQ

Common Questions

Are these final buying prices?

No. They are indicative estimates only. Final pricing depends on inspection, quality, logistics and market conditions.

Are the rates live?

Copper, aluminium and lead can come from your live API. Brass can be derived from copper/zinc reference values. Steel and iron can be configured as UAE scrap reference values unless you connect a specialist scrap-price API.

Can customers submit enquiries?

Yes. The form is ready for Netlify Forms and will capture name, phone, email, location, material and quantity.
