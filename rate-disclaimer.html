Rate Disclaimer

Scrap prices are estimates, not guaranteed offers.

Last updated: 29 June 2026

The calculator on Metal Scrap UAE is designed to provide indicative UAE scrap metal estimates. Rates shown on this website are guide values only and should not be treated as a confirmed buying price, selling price, contract, or commercial offer.

Why final prices may change

A final scrap quote can change after physical inspection, weighing, sorting, moisture checks, metal grade verification, contamination checks, pickup location, loading access, transport cost, buyer demand, and daily market movement.

Clean, mixed, and low grade material

Grade labels are simplified for calculator use. Actual buyer grading may be more detailed. Clean material is usually sorted and mostly free from attachments. Mixed material may need extra processing. Low grade material may contain insulation, corrosion, plastic, rubber, oil, paint, or mixed metals.

Currency conversion

Currency conversion is provided for convenience. Exchange rates may come from a free third-party source or stored fallback values if the source is unavailable. The AED guide rate remains the base value used by the calculator.

Requesting a quote

To receive a clearer estimate, use the Request a Scrap Quote form and include the material type, estimated weight, emirate, pickup location, and any useful notes about the condition of the scrap.
