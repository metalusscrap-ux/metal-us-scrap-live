Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Metal Scrap UAE operates the website at metalusscrap.com. This policy explains what information may be collected when you use the calculator, submit a quote request, or interact with advertising and analytics services that may be added to the site.

Information you provide

If you request a scrap quote, we may receive your name, phone number, email address, emirate, metal type, quantity, message, and the calculator estimate summary. This information is used to respond to your request.

Calculator data

Calculator inputs are processed in your browser. The site does not need an account or payment details to calculate an indicative estimate.

Third-party services

This site may use third-party services for form handling, currency data, advertising, analytics, security, and hosting. These services may process technical information such as IP address, browser details, and cookies according to their own policies.

Advertising cookies

If Google AdSense or another ad network is enabled, advertising partners may use cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, or similar technologies to serve, measure, and personalize ads. You can control cookies through your browser settings and any consent controls displayed on the site.

Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to show ads based on previous visits to this or other websites. Visitors can manage personalized advertising through Google's ad settings, browser cookie controls, and participating vendor opt-out tools.

You can read more about how Google uses information from partner sites at Google's partner site data policy.

Data retention

Quote request messages may be retained as long as needed to answer the query, manage customer communication, prevent spam, and maintain business records.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact contact@metalusscrap.com.
